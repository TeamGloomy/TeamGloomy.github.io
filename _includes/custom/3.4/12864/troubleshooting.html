## Troubleshooting If the screen is showing artifacts/random characters on the screen, the following may improve/eliminate the issue * Lower the SPI frequency by half. This is the F value in M918. * Reduce the length of the cable between the screen and the board. * Ensure that the cable between the screen and the board is routed away from other cables, especially motor cables. * Add a ferrite ring to the cable between the screen and the board.
